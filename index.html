SLIIT Staff Development Programme

Staff Development Programme is conducted by Academic Development and Quality Assurance Unit of Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology (SLIIT) for a period of 150 hours is aimed at improving and enhancing knowledge of academics on Quality assurance in higher education, pedagogy of teaching, professionalism and personality, and Soft Skills development. Resource persons from various academic institutions and expert areas were invited to share their experiences and knowledge with the participants.
This Programme is organized for the 5th consecutive time with 32 participants from SLIIT Malabe campus, SLIIT metro campus and CINEC campus, where I had the honor of being a participant too. I would like to express my my sincere and deep sense of gratitude for Professor Colin Peiris for providing this precious opportunity for us.
